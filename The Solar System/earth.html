Third planet from the Sun

The home of us, and all known living beings in the entire Universe. Also the only observed place to currently hold liquid water on its surface. Due to Earths uncommon axial tilt, the planet has prominent seasons affecting local the temperature and weather based on the time of the year. Because of Earths weather and geological activity, Earth also has by far the most complex surface texture of any planet, with its sharp mountain peaks, valleys and hills. Earth is also the biggest inner planet and the planet with the highest density.

History and Future

The Earth formed around 4.5 billions years ago along with the rest of the Solar System. Due to all the in falling material, Earth was much hotter back then, and the surface was mostly molten lava. There is also a well-known hypothesis that during these early periods the Earth collided with another smaller planet called Theia, combining to form a bigger planet, with the debris forming our Moon.
